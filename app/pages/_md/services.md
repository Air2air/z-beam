# Our Services

Z-Beam delivers cutting-edge laser cleaning solutions tailored to your specific industrial needs. Our non-contact cleaning technology offers precision, efficiency, and environmental benefits that traditional cleaning methods cannot match.

## Core Services

### Surface Preparation

Our laser surface preparation services create the optimal surface conditions for subsequent manufacturing processes:

- **Adhesive Bonding Preparation**: Achieve superior bond strength by creating the ideal surface energy and roughness profile.
- **Pre-Welding Cleaning**: Remove contaminants and oxides to produce stronger, defect-free welds.
- **Pre-Coating Preparation**: Ensure maximum coating adhesion and performance through precise surface preparation.
- **Micro-Texturing**: Create engineered surface patterns that enhance functional properties.

### Oxide & Contamination Removal

Our precision laser cleaning effectively removes unwanted materials:

- **Oxide Layer Removal**: Selectively remove oxide layers from metal surfaces without affecting the base material.
- **Rust Removal**: Efficiently eliminate rust from steel and iron components with precise control.
- **Oil & Grease Removal**: Remove organic contaminants without chemicals or solvents.
- **Particulate Removal**: Clean sensitive surfaces of micro-particles and dust.

### Coating & Paint Removal

Precision removal of coatings with unmatched control:

- **Selective Layer Removal**: Remove specific coating layers without affecting underlying materials.
- **Paint Stripping**: Efficiently remove paint from metal, composite, and plastic substrates.
- **Thermal Barrier Coating Removal**: Precision removal for aerospace and turbine applications.
- **Mold Release Agent Removal**: Prepare surfaces for secondary operations.

### Restoration & Cleaning

Restore components to like-new condition:

- **Historical Artifact Preservation**: Gentle cleaning of sensitive historical items.
- **Mold & Tool Cleaning**: Extend the life of production tooling through regular maintenance.
- **Electronics Cleaning**: Precision cleaning of circuit boards and electronic components.
- **Architectural Surface Restoration**: Clean stone, metal, and glass surfaces without damage.

## Industry Solutions

### Aerospace

Our aerospace laser cleaning services meet the demanding requirements of aircraft manufacturers and MRO facilities:

- **Composite Surface Preparation**: Prepare CFRP and other composites for bonding or repair
- **Engine Component Cleaning**: Remove carbon deposits and oxides from turbine components
- **Landing Gear Restoration**: Clean and prepare critical load-bearing components
- **Aircraft Structure Preparation**: Prepare aluminum, titanium, and steel structures for treatment

### Automotive

Precision cleaning solutions for automotive manufacturing and restoration:

- **Body Panel Preparation**: Prepare metal and composite panels for painting
- **Powertrain Component Cleaning**: Clean engine blocks, transmission cases, and drivetrain components
- **Mold Cleaning**: Maintain injection and compression molds for optimal part quality
- **Vintage Car Restoration**: Gentle cleaning of irreplaceable classic car components

### Electronics

Ultra-precise cleaning for sensitive electronic components:

- **PCB Cleaning**: Remove flux, contamination, and coatings from circuit boards
- **Connector Preparation**: Clean contact surfaces for optimal electrical connections
- **Semiconductor Processing**: Prepare wafers and components for manufacturing steps
- **Microelectronics Cleaning**: Precision cleaning of miniature components

### Medical

Meeting the stringent requirements of medical device manufacturing:

- **Implant Preparation**: Create biocompatible surfaces for medical implants
- **Surgical Instrument Cleaning**: Precision cleaning of complex surgical tools
- **Medical Mold Maintenance**: Clean and prepare molds for medical device production
- **Surface Texturing**: Create functional surfaces for medical applications
