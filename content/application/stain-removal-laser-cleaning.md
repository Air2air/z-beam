name: Laser-Based Stain Removal for Industrial Surface Treatment and Metal Restoration
description: Laser-based stain removal is an advanced surface cleaning technique that
  utilizes high-intensity laser beams to selectively remove stains, oxidation, rust,
  and other surface contaminants without damaging the underlying substrate. This non-contact,
  eco-friendly process is particularly effective for industrial applications requiring
  precision cleaning of metals, composites, and other materials. The technology operates
  through photothermal and photomechanical mechanisms where laser energy is absorbed
  by surface contaminants, causing rapid vaporization or ablation while preserving
  the base material integrity. Applications range from large-scale industrial equipment
  cleaning to delicate restoration of historical artifacts, offering significant advantages
  over traditional chemical or mechanical cleaning methods in terms of precision,
  repeatability, and environmental impact.
