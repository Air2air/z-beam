**Before Cleaning:** The aluminum surface exhibits significant degradation, including: - **Surface contaminants:** Oxidized layers (Al₂O₃) measuring 15-80 μm thickness, industrial particulates (Fe, Si, C compounds), and hydrocarbon films with contact angle >95°. - **Oxide layers:** Naturally occurring passive Al₂O₃ film (~4 nm) with secondary boehmite (AlO(OH)) formation in high-humidity conditions.

**Equipment:** Pulsed Nd:YAG laser system (Quantel Brilliant) - **Wavelength:** 1064 nm (±3 nm) - **Parameters:** 1.8 J/cm² fluence, 8 ns pulse duration, 20 Hz repetition rate, 0.5 mm spot diameter (TEM₀₀ mode).
