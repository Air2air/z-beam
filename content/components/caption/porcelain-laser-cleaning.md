## Caption for Porcelain

**Results:**
1. **Likely Contaminants:** Photoresist residues (semiconductor), surface deposits (heritage), or alumina-silicate debris (aerospace).

3. **Surface Properties:** 0.01µm Ra finish (SEM analysis), 100% glaze retention (FTIR).
4. **Material Integrity:** 50–500W pulsed laser (10–100ns) prevents microcracks in the 90–99% silica matrix (thermal shock resistance).

1. **Power Settings:** 1064nm ±2nm wavelength, 20–200kHz repetition rate, modular 50–500W power.
