**Zirconium** surface (left) before cleaning, showing a thick, tenacious oxide layer (ZrO₂) and embedded particulate contamination from industrial handling.

**After laser cleaning** (right) processed with a 1064 nm Nd:YAG laser at 8 J/cm², 100 ns pulse duration, and a 0.5 mm spot size, resulting in a pristine, oxide-free surface with restored metallic luster and no substrate damage.
