**Kevlar-Reinforced Polymer** surface (left) before cleaning, showing heavy, cross-linked silicone mold release residues and carbonaceous particulate entrained within the aramid fiber weave.
**After laser cleaning** (right) using a 1,064 nm Nd:YAG laser at 150 mJ/cm² fluence and 100 ns pulse duration, which selectively ablated the contaminants while preserving the integrity of the underlying polymer matrix and Kevlar fibers.
