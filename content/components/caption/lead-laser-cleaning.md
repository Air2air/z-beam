**Lead** surface (left) before cleaning, showing heavy, stratified oxide layers (PbO and PbO₂) and embedded particulate contamination.

**After laser cleaning** (right) using a 1064 nm Nd:YAG laser at 8 J/cm², 100 ns pulse duration, and a 5 mm spot size, resulting in the complete removal of reactive compounds and restoration of the pristine metallic substrate without thermal distortion.
