**Results:** Laser cleaning removed surface contaminants (carbon deposits, mineral accretions) from stoneware, reducing oxide layers by 95% while maintaining surface integrity. Post-treatment analysis showed Ra improved from 12.3µm to 1.8µm, with contact angle reduction from 110° to 35° (enhanced wettability).  

**Equipment:** Q-switched Nd:YAG laser (1064nm ±2nm) operated at 0.5–12 J/cm² fluence, 10–100ns pulse duration. Modular 50–500W system with 0.1–5mm spot size achieved precise ablation without substrate damage.
