**Results:** Laser cleaning removed 99.9% of oxide layers and organic contaminants (SEM-verified) from Silicon Nitride while maintaining <0.1µm surface roughness (profilometry). Wettability improved by 40° contact angle reduction without thermal shock (1900°C melting point preserved).  

**Equipment:** Q-switched Nd:YAG laser (1064nm ±2nm) operated at 0.5–10 J/cm² fluence, 5–50ns pulses, 20–200W power. Spot size adjusted 0.05–3mm for precision ablation, with closed-loop chiller ensuring thermal stability.
