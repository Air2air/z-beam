* **Material Properties & Laser Compatibility**: Silicon Carbide (SiC) exhibits exceptional hardness (9.5 Mohs, 2600-3000 kg/mm² Vickers) and high thermal conductivity (120-490 W/m·K). Optimal laser cleaning uses Q-switched Nd:YAG or fiber lasers at 1064 nm (IR) or 532 nm (green) with fluence ranges of 2.5-8.0 J/cm² for precise ablation.
* **Industrial Applications**: Ideal for semiconductor wafer cleaning (<0.1μm roughness preservation), aerospace turbine blade refurbishment (10-20W power), and automotive brake disc degreasing (98.7% contaminant removal). Compatible with hybrid materials like Al₂O₃ and graphite for co-cleaning.
* **Process Specifications**: Operates at 10-200W pulsed power, 10-100 ns pulse duration, and 20-100 kHz repetition rate. Adjustable spot size (50-200 μm) ensures controlled removal rates (0.05-0.2 μm/pass) with minimal thermal damage (<5μm HAZ).
* **Environmental & Regulatory Benefits**: Eliminates 100% solvent use, reduces energy consumption by 40-60%, and cuts hazardous waste by 90% vs. chemical methods. Complies with ISO 18554:2016, SEMI F47-0706, and ASTM C1421-18 standards.
