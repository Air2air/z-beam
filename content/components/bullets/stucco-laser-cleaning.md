Laser cleaning effectively removes dirt, soot, and biological growth from stucco surfaces without damaging the substrate. Optimal laser parameters for stucco cleaning typically range between 1064 nm wavelength and 10-20 ns pulse duration. The process generates minimal hazardous waste compared to chemical or abrasive cleaning methods. Laser-treated stucco exhibits improved adhesion for subsequent coatings or repairs due to surface activation.
