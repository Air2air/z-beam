# Content configuration
---

Laser cleaning of Terra Cotta requires precise control of fluence (0.5-2 J/cm²) to avoid surface vitrification. Optimal wavelength is 1064 nm with pulse durations of 10-100 ns. The material's porosity (15-25%) affects cleaning efficiency and requires adjustable spot sizes (0.1-2 mm). Post-cleaning surface analysis should include spectrophotometry for color consistency verification.
