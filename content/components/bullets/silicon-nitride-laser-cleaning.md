* **Material Properties**: Silicon Nitride (Si₃N₄) exhibits exceptional thermal stability (decomposes at 1900°C), high hardness (15-18 GPa Vickers), and low thermal expansion (3.2×10⁻⁶/°C), making it ideal for precision laser cleaning. Its density ranges from 3.17-3.35 g/cm³, and it has a thermal conductivity of 20-30 W/m·K.
* **Laser Processing Parameters**: Optimized for Nd:YAG or Fiber lasers at 1064 nm (primary) or 532 nm (harmonic), with a fluence range of 2.5-8.0 J/cm². Key specifications include pulsed power (50-300W), pulse duration (10-100 ns), adjustable spot size (50-200 μm), and repetition rate (20-100 kHz).
* **Key Applications**: Precision cleaning in semiconductor wafer handling (±0.1 μm tolerance), aerospace bearing surfaces (15,000 RPM operation), and medical implant sterilization. Compatible with stainless steel, aluminum oxide, and titanium alloys for hybrid components.
* **Performance & Environmental Benefits**: Achieves 99.9% contaminant removal (verified by FTIR), reduces surface roughness (Ra 0.8 μm to 0.2 μm), and processes at 15 cm²/min. Environmentally superior, eliminating 95% of solvent use and consuming 40% less energy than thermal methods.
