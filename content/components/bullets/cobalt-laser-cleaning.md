* **Material Properties**: Cobalt (Co) is a hard, lustrous, and magnetic transition metal with high thermal and electrical conductivity, making it suitable for precision laser cleaning applications where controlled thermal processing is critical.
* **Laser Parameters**: Optimal laser cleaning of cobalt typically employs a **pulsed fiber laser at 1064nm wavelength**, balancing ablation efficiency with minimal thermal damage to the substrate, ensuring precise removal of surface contaminants.
* **Applications**: Ideal for **non-contact cleaning** of cobalt composites in aerospace, medical devices, and electronics, where surface restoration and contamination removal are essential without compromising material integrity.
* **Benefits**: Laser cleaning offers **selective material removal**, reduced chemical waste, and enhanced precision compared to traditional methods, making it a sustainable solution for maintaining cobalt components in industrial settings.
