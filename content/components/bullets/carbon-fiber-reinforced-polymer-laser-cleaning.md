* **Material Composition & Properties**: CFRP consists of 50-70% carbon fibers and 30-50% epoxy resin, with a density of 1.5-1.6 g/cm³ and anisotropic thermal conductivity (5-50 W/m·K). The polymer matrix has a melting point of 300-400°C, and the composite exhibits a hardness of 3-4 Mohs, making it ideal for high-strength applications.
* **Laser Cleaning Parameters**: Optimized for pulsed fiber lasers (1064 nm ±2 nm) with fluence ranges of 0.5-10 J/cm², pulse durations of 10-200 ns, and power outputs of 20-500W. Key metrics include >99.5% contaminant removal, <0.1% fiber damage, and cleaning rates of 0.5-2 m²/hour for aerospace components.
* **Applications & Compatibility**: Widely used in aerospace (paint/oxide removal), automotive (mold cleaning), and wind energy (blade maintenance). Compatible with aluminum alloys, titanium, and thermoplastics for hybrid structures, enabling non-destructive surface pretreatment.
* **Environmental & Operational Benefits**: Reduces solvent use by 95%, extends component lifespan by 30-50%, and consumes 40-60% less energy than abrasive methods. Complies with ISO 14001:2015 and EN 60825-1 standards, ensuring sustainable and safe laser processing.
