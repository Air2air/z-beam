1. **Technical Focus: Material-specific laser parameters** - The optimal laser parameters for CFRP ablation, widely adopted in local high-precision electronics manufacturing, utilize a 1064 nm wavelength with a fluence range of 0.5-1.5 J/cm² and nanosecond pulse durations to effectively remove epoxy resin while preserving the integrity of the underlying carbon fibers.
2. **Technical Focus: Industrial applications** - This non-contact process is predominantly applied for precision surface preparation of CFRP components within the semiconductor supply chain, including the cleaning of wafer handling equipment and delicate robotic end-effectors prior to bonding or coating operations.
3. **Technical Focus: Safety considerations** - Stringent protocols, aligned with local industrial safety regulations, mandate the use of Class 1 enclosures with integrated fume extraction to mitigate risks from airborne particulates and volatile organic compounds released during the resin removal process.
4. **Technical Focus: Environmental benefits** - Laser cleaning presents a significant ecological advantage by eliminating the need for chemical solvents and abrasive media, thereby reducing hazardous waste streams and supporting the region's green manufacturing initiatives for a more sustainable electronics sector.
