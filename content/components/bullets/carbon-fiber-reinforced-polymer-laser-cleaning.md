* **Optimal Laser Parameters**: Utilizes pulsed fiber/Q-switched Nd:YAG lasers at 1064nm wavelength with fluence range of 0.5-5 J/cm², pulse duration of 10-200ns, and spot size of 50-200μm for precise ablation. Achieves 99.5% contaminant removal with <0.5% fiber damage, as per ASTM E2108 and ISO 13067 standards.
* **Material Composition & Properties**: Comprises 60-70% carbon fiber and 30-40% epoxy resin (C₁₁H₁₂O₃)ₙ, with density of 1.5-1.6 g/cm³ and anisotropic thermal conductivity (5-50 W/m·K). Polymer matrix has a melting point of 350-400°C, requiring controlled laser energy to avoid thermal damage.
* **Key Applications**: Aerospace component refurbishment (2.5 J/cm² for 99.7% residue removal), automotive composite repair (30W laser, 50μm spot size), and wind turbine blade maintenance (1.2 J/cm², 2.5 m²/hour processing rate). Compatible with aluminum alloys and titanium for hybrid structures.
* **Environmental & Regulatory Benefits**: Reduces solvent use by 98%, energy consumption by 35-40%, and waste by 90% compared to traditional methods. Complies with ISO 18473-3:2018 for laser processing and EN 60825-1:2014 for laser safety (Class 4 IEC).
