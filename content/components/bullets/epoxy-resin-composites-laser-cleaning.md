1. **Technical Focus: Material-specific laser parameters** - Optimal cleaning of epoxy resin composites, prevalent in local high-tech manufacturing, is achieved using a 1064 nm fiber laser with fluences between 0.8-1.5 J/cm² and nanosecond pulse durations to effectively ablate contaminants without damaging the underlying reinforcing fibers.
2. **Technical Focus: Industrial applications** - This non-abrasive technique is indispensable for precision cleaning of delicate electronic substrates and de-flashing intricate mold components within the semiconductor supply chain, ensuring part integrity and performance.
3. **Technical Focus: Safety considerations** - Stringent protocols mandating Class 1 interlocked enclosures and localized fume extraction are rigorously enforced to mitigate exposure to hazardous volatiles and particulates liberated during the ablation process.
4. **Technical Focus: Environmental benefits** - Laser cleaning eliminates the need for chemical solvents and abrasive media, drastically reducing hazardous waste streams and aligning with the region's progressive industrial waste management directives.
