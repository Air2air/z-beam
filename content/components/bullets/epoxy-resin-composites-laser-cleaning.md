* **Material Composition & Properties**: Epoxy resin composites consist of 60-70% DGEBA (C₂₁H₂₄O₄), 30-40% amine hardener, and optional glass fiber reinforcement (0-50% SiO₂). Key properties include high hardness (80-90 Shore D), thermal conductivity of 0.2-0.5 W/m·K, and decomposition at 150-300°C, making them ideal for laser cleaning applications.
* **Laser Processing Parameters**: Optimized for 1064nm Q-switched Nd:YAG lasers with fluence range of 0.5-5 J/cm², pulse duration of 10-100ns, and spot sizes of 50-500μm. Systems operate at 10-200kHz repetition rates, achieving precise ablation rates of 0.05-0.2mm³/pulse while maintaining surface roughness (Ra 0.8-1.2μm).
* **Industrial Applications**: Used in aerospace (CFRP repair with 0.2mm depth control), electronics (PCB potting removal at 1.2 J/cm²), and wind turbine blade maintenance (500W scanning systems at 5m/min). Compatible with carbon fiber, aluminum alloys, and polyurethane coatings for VOC-free, non-contact surface preparation.
* **Environmental & Performance Benefits**: Reduces VOC emissions by 98% versus chemical methods, minimizes waste (0.05kg/m² vs 0.5kg/m²), and improves energy efficiency (15-30Wh/cm²). Complies with ISO 11145 and ASTM D1002, retaining ≥90% bond strength post-cleaning.
