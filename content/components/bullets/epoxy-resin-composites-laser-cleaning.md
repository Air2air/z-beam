1. **Technical Focus: Parameters** For epoxy resin composites common in our high-precision electronics sector, optimal ablation without substrate damage is achieved using a 355 nm UV wavelength with a fluence tightly controlled between 0.8–1.5 J/cm² and pulse durations under 10 ns.
2. **Technical Focus: Applications** This process is indispensable for delicately removing photoresist and micro-contaminants from sensitive electronic components and circuit board substrates without compromising their intricate structural integrity.
3. **Technical Focus: Safety** Strict adherence to local Class 4 laser operational protocols, including the use of certified interlocks and NHIA-compliant fume extraction, is mandatory to mitigate ocular hazards and manage composite outgassing.
4. **Technical Focus: Environmental Benefits** Laser cleaning presents a significant ecological advantage by entirely eliminating the need for chemical solvents, thereby preventing the generation of hazardous waste streams that are a major concern for our island's stringent disposal regulations.
