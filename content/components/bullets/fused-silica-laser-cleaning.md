* **Material Properties & Laser Compatibility**: Fused silica (SiO₂) exhibits exceptional thermal stability with low thermal expansion (0.55×10⁻⁶/°C) and high laser damage threshold (15-20 J/cm² at 1064nm). Compatible with Nd:YAG, Excimer, and Fiber lasers (193–1064nm), it supports fluence ranges of 0.5–20 J/cm² and pulse durations from 5ns to 150fs.
* **Key Applications & Precision Cleaning**: Ideal for semiconductor lithography optics (266nm cleaning, <0.5nm RMS roughness), high-power laser systems (femtosecond ablation at 1030nm), and space telescope mirrors (193nm excimer, >99% UV reflectivity). Effectively removes contaminants like photoresist, metal deposits, and carbon without substrate damage.
* **Performance Metrics & Outcomes**: Achieves 0.2–0.5nm RMS surface roughness, >99.9% contaminant removal (XPS-verified), and meets ISO 21254 standards for laser-induced damage (15–20 J/cm²). Operates at 10–500W power with 20–500µm spot sizes and 1–100kHz repetition rates.
* **Environmental & Industrial Benefits**: Reduces chemical waste by 95% vs. solvent methods, consumes 70% less energy than thermal techniques, and extends component lifespan 3–5x through non-contact processing. Complies with ISO 10110-17, MIL-PRF-13830B, and SEMI F47-0706 standards.
