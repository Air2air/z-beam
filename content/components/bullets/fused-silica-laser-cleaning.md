* **High Laser Transparency & Damage Threshold**: Fused silica (SiO₂) exhibits exceptional transparency at 1064nm, making it ideal for pulsed fiber laser cleaning. Its high damage threshold ensures minimal material degradation during laser ablation, even at high fluence levels.
* **Non-Contact Precision Cleaning**: Laser cleaning of fused silica enables non-contact removal of surface contaminants (e.g., oxides, particulates) without mechanical stress, preserving substrate integrity and optical clarity.
* **Thermal Stability & Low Thermal Expansion**: With near-zero thermal expansion and high thermal shock resistance, fused silica withstands rapid temperature changes during laser processing, reducing cracking or deformation risks.
* **Industrial Applications**: Widely used in optics, semiconductors, and aerospace for restoring high-purity surfaces, fused silica laser cleaning is critical for removing coatings, residues, and micro-contaminants without chemical solvents.
