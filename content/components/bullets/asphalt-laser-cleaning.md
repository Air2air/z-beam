* **Optical & Thermal Parameters**: Utilizes Nd:YAG or Fiber lasers (1064 nm IR or 532 nm green) with fluence range of 0.8–3.5 J/cm², optimized for asphalt's thermal conductivity (0.75–1.05 W/m·K) and softening point (150–200°C). Pulse durations of 10–100 ns and spot sizes of 0.2–5 mm ensure precise bitumen removal without aggregate damage.
* **Composition & Compatibility**: Targets asphalt's composite structure (4–8% bitumen CₓHᵧSᵤ, 92–96% mineral aggregates like SiO₂/CaCO₃) for applications including road marking restoration, bridge deck preparation, and joint cleaning with concrete or steel substrates.
* **Environmental & Operational Benefits**: Achieves >98% contaminant removal (FTIR-verified) while reducing water usage by 100% (vs. pressure washing) and CO₂ emissions by 60%. Integrated vacuum systems capture 100% of debris, complying with ASTM D8-20 and ISO 18513 standards.
* **Industrial Applications**: Processes 2–5 m²/hour for road surface restoration (2.1 J/cm² fluence), airport runway maintenance (100W fiber laser at 20kHz), and parking structure rehab (532 nm for oil stains), preserving surface roughness (Ra <0.5μm change).
