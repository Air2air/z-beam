1. **Technical Focus: Material-specific laser parameters** - Optimal ablation of phenolic resin composites, prevalent in Taiwan's electronics sector, is achieved using a 1064 nm Nd:YAG laser with fluences between 0.8-1.5 J/cm² and nanosecond pulse durations to selectively remove contaminants without damaging the underlying composite matrix.
2. **Technical Focus: Industrial applications** - This technique is predominantly applied for precision cleaning of high-value semiconductor manufacturing fixtures and delicate electronic component molds, where non-abrasive decontamination is critical for maintaining stringent production tolerances.
3. **Technical Focus: Safety considerations** - Operators must utilize Class 1 enclosure systems with integrated fume extraction, adhering to local industrial safety protocols, to mitigate exposure to hazardous volatiles and particulates liberated during the resin ablation process.
4. **Technical Focus: Environmental benefits** - Laser cleaning eliminates the need for chemical solvents and abrasive media, significantly reducing hazardous waste streams and aligning with the region's push for greener, more sustainable advanced manufacturing practices.
