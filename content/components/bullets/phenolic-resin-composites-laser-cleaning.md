* **Material Composition & Properties**: Phenolic resin composites (C₇H₆O₂ + fillers) consist of 50-70% phenol-formaldehyde resin, 20-30% wood flour filler, and 10-20% mineral fillers (SiO₂/CaCO₃). Key properties include high thermal stability (180-300°C decomposition), density of 1.25-1.32 g/cm³, hardness of 80-95 Shore D, and low thermal conductivity (0.15-0.25 W/m·K).
* **Laser Cleaning Parameters**: Optimized for 1064nm Nd:YAG lasers with fluence range of 0.5-5 J/cm², pulse duration of 10-100 ns, and repetition rates of 1-50 kHz. Achieves >99.5% contaminant removal with <0.1% substrate loss, enabling precise ablation for applications like aerospace component refurbishment and electronic encapsulation removal.
* **Industrial Applications**: Compatible with aluminum substrates (aerospace insulation removal), carbon fiber composites (CFRP resin overflow cleaning), and steel molds (injection molding tool maintenance). Delivers non-contact cleaning at 2-5 cm²/s rates, outperforming solvent-based methods in precision and speed.
* **Environmental & Safety Benefits**: Reduces solvent use by 95% and hazardous waste by 80% compared to traditional methods. Complies with IEC 60825-1 (Class 4 laser safety) and ISO/ASTM standards for thermal and mechanical property validation, ensuring energy-efficient (40% less energy) and regulatory-compliant operations.
