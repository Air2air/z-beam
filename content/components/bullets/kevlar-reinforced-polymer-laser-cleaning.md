* **Material Composition & Properties**: Composed of 55-65% Kevlar fibers (C₁₄H₁₀N₂O₂) in an epoxy resin matrix (30-40%), with a density of 1.44 g/cm³ and exceptional thermal stability (decomposes at 500°C). Low thermal conductivity (0.04 W/m·K) and 3.5 Mohs hardness make it ideal for laser cleaning without structural compromise.
* **Laser Cleaning Parameters**: Optimized for 1064nm Nd:YAG lasers with 0.5-5 J/cm² fluence, 10-100ns pulse duration, and 20-100kHz repetition rate. Spot sizes of 0.5-2.0mm ensure precision, achieving >99% contaminant removal while preserving >98% tensile strength.
* **Key Applications**: Aerospace component refurbishment (oxidation removal), ballistic armor maintenance (99.7% contaminant removal per ASTM E1226), and automotive composite repair (40% bond strength improvement vs. mechanical methods). Compatible with aluminum, carbon fiber, and stainless steel substrates.
* **Environmental & Performance Benefits**: Reduces solvent use (5-7 liters/cm² eliminated), consumes 75% less energy (0.3kWh/m²), and produces 92% less waste than sandblasting. Achieves 2.5m²/hour processing speed at 100W with <0.5% fiber damage.
