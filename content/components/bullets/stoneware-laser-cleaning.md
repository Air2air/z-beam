1. **Technical Focus:** Material-specific laser parameters for stoneware, a dense, vitrified ceramic with low porosity, typically employ a 1064 nm Nd:YAG laser with a fluence range of 1.2-2.5 J/cm² and nanosecond pulses to effectively ablate contaminants without damaging the silicate matrix.
2. **Technical Focus:** Key applications include precision cleaning of stoneware substrates and components within the electronics supply chain, particularly for removing oxides and micro-debris prior to high-value semiconductor packaging and assembly processes.
3. **Technical Focus:** Safety considerations mandate Class 1 enclosures with interlocks for all stationary systems, supplemented by mandatory PPE including certified laser safety goggles for the 1064 nm wavelength and robust fume extraction to handle ejected ceramic particulates.
4. **Technical Focus:** The environmental benefit is a drastic reduction in chemical solvent usage and wastewater generation, aligning with local waste management priorities by eliminating the need for neutralization and disposal of harsh acidic cleaning agents.
