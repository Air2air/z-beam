2. **Technical Focus: Applications** Within the local high-tech manufacturing sector, this process is critical for precision cleaning of TPE seals and gaskets on semiconductor fabrication equipment and for preparing connector surfaces in electronics assembly.
3. **Technical Focus: Safety** Stringent protocols mandating Class 1 enclosure integration and localized exhaust ventilation are non-negotiable to mitigate fume inhalation risks and comply with regional industrial hygiene standards for airborne particulates.
4. **Technical Focus: Environmental Benefits** This dry process eliminates the need for chemical solvents and abrasive media, drastically reducing hazardous waste streams and supporting the local industry's shift towards greener, more sustainable manufacturing practices.
