* **Material Composition & Properties**: Polyester resin composites (C₁₀H₈O₄)ₙ consist of 50-70% polymer matrix, 20-40% glass fiber reinforcement (SiO₂), and 5-15% calcium carbonate filler. Key properties include density (1.2-1.5 g/cm³), hardness (80-85 Shore D), and thermal conductivity (0.17-0.25 W/m·K), with decomposition at 220-260°C.
* **Laser Cleaning Parameters**: Optimal laser cleaning requires precise control of wavelength (1064nm Nd:YAG or 10,600nm CO₂), fluence (0.5-8 J/cm²), and pulse duration (10-150ns). Typical settings include 50-500W pulsed power, 0.1-2.0mm spot size, and 10-100kHz repetition rate for non-contact surface preparation.
* **Industrial Applications**: Used in marine composite restoration (2-4 J/cm² for fouling removal), aerospace component refurbishment (1-3 J/cm² for adhesive bonding prep), and automotive mold maintenance (50-100W Nd:YAG for release agent cleaning). Compatible with carbon steel, aluminum, and fiberglass substrates.
* **Environmental & Performance Benefits**: Reduces solvent use by 95-100%, generates 80% less waste than mechanical methods, and improves energy efficiency (30-40% savings). Outcomes include surface roughness (Ra 1.2-2.5μm), 45-60% adhesion improvement, and 0.5-2.0 m²/hour processing speed. Complies with ISO 11357-3 and EN 60825-1 standards.
