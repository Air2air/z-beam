1. **Technical Focus: Material-specific laser parameters** For Taiwan's electronics mold maintenance, optimal cleaning of polyester resin composites is achieved using a 1064 nm fiber laser with a fluence of 1.2-1.8 J/cm² and nanosecond pulses to selectively ablate contaminants without damaging the underlying composite matrix.
2. **Technical Focus: Industrial applications** This precise laser ablation process is critical for delicately restoring intricate injection molds used in the local semiconductor packaging industry, removing built-up resin residues to ensure flawless component encapsulation.
3. **Technical Focus: Safety considerations** Operators must utilize Class 1 enclosure systems with integrated fume extraction that meets local workplace air quality standards, as the process can generate volatile organic compounds and particulate matter.
4. **Technical Focus: Environmental benefits** The technique eliminates the need for chemical solvents and abrasive media, significantly reducing hazardous waste streams and aligning with the region's stringent industrial waste management protocols.
