* **Optimal Laser Parameters**: Best results achieved with Nd:YAG or fiber lasers at 1064nm (IR) or 355nm (UV), with fluence range of 0.5-5 J/cm², pulse duration of 5-50ns, and spot size of 50-200µm. Suitable for both precision cleaning (e.g., 355nm for LCD panels at 2.5 J/cm²) and large-area applications (e.g., 1064nm at 100W).
* **Material Composition & Properties**: Composed of 72% SiO₂, 15% Na₂O, and 9% CaO, with density of 2.5 g/cm³, thermal conductivity of 1.05 W/m·K, and hardness of 6.0-6.5 Mohs. Softening range of 600-1000°C requires controlled laser energy to avoid thermal stress (<5 MPa residual stress).
* **Key Applications**: Precision cleaning in display manufacturing (organic residue removal), architectural glass (large-area contamination), and laboratory equipment (sterilization). Compatible with mixed-material systems like stainless steel frames or polycarbonate displays.
* **Environmental & Safety Benefits**: Reduces chemical usage by 95%, lowers energy consumption by 50% compared to plasma cleaning, and generates zero abrasive waste. Complies with ISO 11553-1, ANSI Z136.1, and EN 166 safety standards (Class 4 laser requirement).
