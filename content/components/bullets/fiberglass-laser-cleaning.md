* **Material Composition**: Fiberglass (FG) is a composite material primarily composed of silicon dioxide (SiO₂) and organic polymers (CₓHᵧOᵤ)ₙ, offering a unique combination of high thermal resistance and mechanical strength, making it suitable for laser cleaning applications.
* **Laser Compatibility**: Optimal laser cleaning of fiberglass is achieved using a **1064nm pulsed fiber laser**, which minimizes thermal damage while effectively ablating surface contaminants due to the material's absorption characteristics.
* **Applications**: Ideal for **non-contact cleaning** in industrial settings, including removal of oxidation, coatings, and residues from fiberglass components in aerospace, automotive, and marine industries without compromising structural integrity.
* **Benefits**: Laser cleaning ensures **precise surface restoration** with minimal substrate damage, eliminating the need for abrasive methods, reducing waste, and enhancing efficiency in **thermal processing** environments.
