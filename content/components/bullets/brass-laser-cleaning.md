* **Material Composition & Suitability**: Brass (Cu₁₋ₓZnₓ) is a copper-zinc alloy with excellent thermal conductivity, making it responsive to laser cleaning. The 1064nm wavelength (pulsed fiber laser) is optimal for controlled ablation without excessive heat input.
* **Key Applications**: Ideal for removing oxidation, tarnish, grease, and surface contaminants in industrial settings, including precision components, musical instruments, and decorative hardware.
* **Process Advantages**: Non-contact laser cleaning preserves brass integrity, avoids mechanical abrasion, and enables selective contamination removal with minimal substrate damage.
* **Optimal Parameters**: Pulsed fiber lasers (1064nm) with adjustable energy density (J/cm²) and pulse duration ensure efficient cleaning while mitigating zinc depletion risks from excessive thermal exposure.
