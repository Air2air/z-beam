The optimal laser parameters for Nickel cleaning typically involve near-infrared wavelengths (1064 nm) with fluence levels between 1-5 J/cm² and pulse durations of 10-100 nanoseconds, carefully calibrated to avoid thermal damage while effectively ablating surface contaminants.
