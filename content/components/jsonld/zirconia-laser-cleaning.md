  "articleBody": "Zirconia (ZrO₂) is an advanced ceramic material prized for its exceptional mechanical strength, high fracture toughness, and excellent thermal stability, making it ideal for demanding industrial applications. Laser cleaning provides a superior non-contact method for removing contaminants, oxide layers, and surface impurities without compromising Zirconia's structural integrity. The process utilizes precisely controlled laser parameters, typically employing nanosecond pulsed lasers at 1064nm wavelength with fluence levels between 1-4 J/cm² and pulse durations of 10-100ns. These parameters enable selective ablation of unwanted surface material while preserving the underlying Zirconia substrate. Key applications include surface preparation for thermal barrier coatings in aerospace components, cleaning of biomedical implants before coating application, and restoration of precision industrial ceramics. Challenges include managing Zirconia's high reflectivity and thermal sensitivity, which are addressed through optimized beam delivery systems and real-time thermal monitoring. Safety considerations require Class 1 laser enclosures, proper fume extraction for nanoparticles, and appropriate personal protective equipment including laser safety glasses. The non-abrasive nature of laser cleaning eliminates mechanical stress and prevents micro-cracking, making it particularly valuable for high-precision Zirconia components in medical and aerospace industries where surface integrity is critical.",
