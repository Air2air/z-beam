articleBody: Urethane composites (UR) are widely used in industrial applications due to their durability and chemical resistance. Laser cleaning offers a non-destructive, precise method for removing contaminants, oxidation, or coatings from urethane composite surfaces without compromising structural integrity. This process is ideal for aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing sectors where material preservation is critical. The technique utilizes controlled laser pulses to vaporize surface impurities while leaving the base material unaffected. High-resolution imaging confirms the effectiveness of laser cleaning on urethane composites, as shown in our detailed case studies.
