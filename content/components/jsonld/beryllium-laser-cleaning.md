articleBody: "Beryllium (Be) is a critical material in aerospace and nuclear industries due to its exceptional strength-to-weight ratio and thermal properties. However, its toxicity demands precise, non-contact cleaning methods. Laser cleaning offers a contamination-free solution, vaporizing oxides and impurities without damaging the base material. This process utilizes pulsed lasers to selectively remove surface contaminants, ensuring optimal performance while minimizing worker exposure to hazardous particles. Applications include satellite components, nuclear reactors, and high-precision instrumentation. The technique preserves beryllium's structural integrity, making it indispensable for industries requiring ultra-clean metal surfaces."
