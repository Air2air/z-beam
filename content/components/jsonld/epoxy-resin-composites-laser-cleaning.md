articleBody: Epoxy resin composites (EP) are widely used in aerospace, automotive, and industrial applications due to their high strength-to-weight ratio and chemical resistance. Laser cleaning offers a non-abrasive, precise method for removing contaminants, oxidation layers, or coatings from epoxy surfaces without compromising structural integrity. This technique utilizes controlled laser pulses to vaporize unwanted material while preserving the underlying composite matrix. Optimal wavelengths and pulse durations are selected based on the filler materials present in the epoxy resin formulation. The process is environmentally friendly, eliminating the need for chemical solvents or mechanical abrasion.
