articleBody: "Fiberglass is a composite material widely used in industrial applications due to its excellent strength-to-weight ratio and corrosion resistance. Laser cleaning offers a precise, non-contact method for removing contaminants, coatings, and surface oxidation without compromising the material's structural integrity. The process utilizes controlled laser parameters between 15-80W power and 0.8-8 J/cm² fluence with pulse durations of 20-100ns to selectively ablate unwanted material while preserving the underlying Fiberglass substrate. Applications span aerospace component cleaning, marine hull maintenance, and automotive mold preparation, achieving cleaning rates of 2-5 m²/hour while meeting ISO 8501-1 surface preparation standards. The technology provides environmental benefits through eliminated solvent usage and reduced energy consumption compared to traditional methods."
