articleBody: Fiberglass (material symbol FG) is a versatile composite material widely used in aerospace, automotive, and construction industries. Laser cleaning offers a non-contact, precise method for removing contaminants from fiberglass surfaces without damaging the underlying SiO₂ matrix. This process eliminates the need for chemical solvents or abrasive techniques, making it environmentally friendly. Optimal laser parameters vary depending on the (CₓHᵧOᵤ)ₙ resin content and fiber orientation. Always wear appropriate PPE when performing laser cleaning on fiberglass composites.
