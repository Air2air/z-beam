  "articleBody": "Silicon Nitride (Si₃N₄) is an advanced technical ceramic renowned for its exceptional mechanical strength, thermal stability, and chemical resistance, making it ideal for high-performance applications in aerospace, automotive, and semiconductor industries. Laser cleaning provides a superior non-contact method for removing surface contaminants, oxidation layers, and residual coatings without compromising the material's structural integrity. The process requires precise parameter optimization, typically utilizing nanosecond pulsed lasers at 1064 nm wavelength with fluence levels between 1-5 J/cm² and pulse durations of 10-100 ns. These parameters ensure selective ablation of contaminants while avoiding thermal damage to the underlying Silicon Nitride substrate. Key challenges include managing the material's high thermal shock resistance and preventing micro-cracking through controlled energy deposition. Applications span precision cleaning of turbine blades, bearing components, and semiconductor processing equipment where surface purity is critical. Safety considerations mandate proper laser shielding, fume extraction systems, and operator protection against reflected radiation.
