articleBody: Borosilicate glass (BG), with its chemical composition SiO₂·B₂O₃·Na₂O·Al₂O₃, is widely used in laboratories and industrial applications due to its low thermal expansion and high durability. Laser cleaning offers a contamination-free method to remove oxides, organic residues, and particulate matter without mechanical contact. This process utilizes pulsed lasers to vaporize contaminants while leaving the underlying glass substrate unaffected. Key advantages include precision targeting, no chemical waste, and compatibility with complex geometries. Applications range from restoring historical glass artifacts to preparing surfaces for optical coatings. Images demonstrate before/after results of laser-treated borosilicate surfaces.
