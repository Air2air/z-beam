articleBody: "Fused silica (SiO₂), a high-purity synthetic glass, is widely used in optics, semiconductors, and aerospace due to its exceptional thermal stability and transparency. Laser cleaning offers a non-contact, precise method to remove surface contaminants like dust, oxides, and organic residues without compromising the material's properties. This process utilizes controlled laser pulses to vaporize impurities while preserving the substrate. Applications include optical lens cleaning, semiconductor wafer preparation, and aerospace component maintenance. The technique is especially valuable for fused silica's sensitivity to mechanical abrasion and chemical etching."
