Laser cleaning offers a precise, non-contact method for removing contaminants, coatings, and surface oxidation without compromising the material\'s structural integrity. The process utilizes controlled laser parameters to selectively ablate unwanted material while preserving the underlying Polyester Resin Composites substrate. Typical processing parameters include 1064nm wavelength at fluences ranging from 0.8-8 J/cm² with pulse durations of 20-100ns. The material\'s density of 1.2-1.5 g/cm³ and thermal conductivity of 0.2-0.3 W/m·K require careful energy management to prevent thermal damage. Applications span aerospace component maintenance, automotive surface preparation, and marine structure preservation. The technology provides environmental benefits by eliminating chemical solvents and reducing energy consumption by 60% compared to traditional methods while achieving contamination removal efficiencies exceeding 99%."
