articleBody: Polyester resin composites (PE) are widely used in industrial applications due to their durability and versatility. Laser cleaning offers a non-abrasive, precise method for removing contaminants, oxidation, and residues from these composites without damaging the underlying material. This technique is particularly effective for maintaining structural integrity while ensuring optimal surface preparation for coatings or bonding. The chemical formula (C₁₀H₈O₄)ₙ + fillers highlights the complex nature of these materials, requiring specialized cleaning approaches. Laser cleaning minimizes waste and eliminates the need for harsh chemicals, making it an environmentally friendly solution for composite maintenance.
