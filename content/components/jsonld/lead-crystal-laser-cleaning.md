articleBody: "Lead crystal, prized for its brilliance and clarity, requires meticulous care to maintain its pristine condition. Traditional cleaning methods risk scratching or chemical etching, but laser cleaning offers a revolutionary alternative. This non-contact process uses precisely calibrated laser beams to remove contaminants like tarnish, dirt, and oxidation without harming the crystal's surface. Ideal for antique chandeliers, fine tableware, and artistic glassware, laser cleaning preserves intricate engravings and optical properties. The technique is environmentally friendly, eliminating the need for harsh solvents. Our process includes pre-assessment with spectral analysis to customize laser parameters for each piece, ensuring safety for high-lead-content glass."
