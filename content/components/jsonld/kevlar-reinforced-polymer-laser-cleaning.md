articleBody: "Kevlar-Reinforced Polymer (KRP), designated by material symbol KV, is a high-performance composite combining aramid fibers with a polymer matrix (chemical formula C₁₄H₁₀N₂O₂ + polymer). Laser cleaning offers a non-contact method to remove contaminants from KRP surfaces while preserving structural integrity. This process selectively ablates residues without thermal degradation of the Kevlar fibers, making it ideal for aerospace and ballistic applications. Pulsed lasers at specific wavelengths (e.g., 1064 nm Nd:YAG) achieve optimal results with energy densities between 2-8 J/cm². The technique eliminates mechanical stress risks associated with traditional methods like sandblasting, maintaining the composite's tensile strength of 3,620 MPa."
