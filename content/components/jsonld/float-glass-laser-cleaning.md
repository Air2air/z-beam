articleBody: Float glass laser cleaning is an innovative technique that uses laser technology to remove contaminants, coatings, and residues from glass surfaces without physical contact. This method is ideal for maintaining the pristine quality of float glass, widely used in construction and automotive industries. Unlike traditional cleaning methods, laser cleaning eliminates the risk of scratches or chemical damage, ensuring long-term durability. The process involves directing a focused laser beam onto the surface, which vaporizes impurities while leaving the glass intact. Benefits include reduced waste, no need for harsh chemicals, and improved efficiency. Applications range from architectural glass restoration to precision cleaning in manufacturing.
