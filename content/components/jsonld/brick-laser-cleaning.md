articleBody: "Brick laser cleaning is a precise, non-destructive method for removing dirt, soot, and biological growth from masonry surfaces. Using targeted laser energy (wavelength 1064 nm), the process vaporizes contaminants without damaging the brick's crystalline structure (Al₂Si₂O₅(OH)₄ + Fe₂O₃ composition). Ideal for historic preservation, this method eliminates mechanical abrasion risks while maintaining the original surface patina. The technique is particularly effective for removing surface deposits and restoring the brick's original appearance."
