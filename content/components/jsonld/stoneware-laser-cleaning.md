articleBody: Stoneware, a robust ceramic material symbolized as SW, benefits significantly from laser cleaning due to its dense composition. Unlike traditional methods, laser cleaning selectively removes dirt, oxides, or biological growth without damaging the stoneware's surface. This process is ideal for historical artifacts or modern stoneware items, ensuring longevity and aesthetic integrity. The technique leverages precise wavelengths to target contaminants while preserving the material's MgO and K₂O-rich matrix. Images included showcase before-and-after results of laser-cleaned stoneware, highlighting its effectiveness. Authored by Evelyn Wu, a Taiwanese expert in ceramic conservation, this guide emphasizes sustainable restoration practices.
