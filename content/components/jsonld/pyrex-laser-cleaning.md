articleBody: Pyrex glass, known for its durability and thermal resistance, requires specialized cleaning methods to maintain its integrity. Laser cleaning offers a non-contact, chemical-free solution that effectively removes contaminants without damaging the surface. This method is ideal for industrial applications where precision and cleanliness are critical. The process involves using focused laser beams to vaporize impurities, leaving the Pyrex surface pristine. Advantages include no abrasive contact, reduced waste, and compatibility with sensitive environments. For optimal results, parameters such as wavelength, pulse duration, and energy density must be carefully calibrated. Images included showcase the before-and-after effects of laser cleaning on Pyrex.
