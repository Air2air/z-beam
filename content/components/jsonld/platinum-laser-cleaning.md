Laser cleaning provides a superior method for removing contaminants, oxides, and surface impurities from Platinum components without mechanical contact or chemical residues. The process utilizes optimized laser parameters including near-infrared wavelengths (1064 nm) with fluence levels between 0.5-2.5 J/cm² and pulse durations of 10-100 nanoseconds. Challenges include managing Platinum's high reflectivity, which requires precise parameter optimization to ensure efficient energy absorption. Safety protocols mandate Class 4 laser safety measures including appropriate eyewear, ventilation for aerosol management, and controlled access to processing areas.
