articleBody: "Phenolic resin composites (PR) are widely used in high-performance applications due to their thermal stability and mechanical strength. Laser cleaning offers a precise, non-contact method for removing contaminants, oxidation layers, or degraded resin without compromising the composite's structural integrity. This process utilizes controlled laser parameters to selectively ablate surface impurities while preserving the underlying C₇H₆O₂ matrix and filler materials. Optimal wavelengths (typically 1064nm or 10.6µm) and pulse durations are critical to prevent thermal degradation. Applications include aerospace component refurbishment, electrical insulation maintenance, and mold cleaning for composite manufacturing."
