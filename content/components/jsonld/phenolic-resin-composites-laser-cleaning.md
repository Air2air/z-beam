articleBody: "Phenolic Resin Composites are high-performance thermoset materials widely used in aerospace, automotive, and industrial applications due to their excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance. Laser cleaning offers a precise, non-contact method for removing contaminants, coatings, and surface oxidation without compromising the material's structural integrity. The process utilizes controlled laser parameters including wavelengths of 1064nm or 532nm, fluence levels of 1.0-8.0 J/cm², and pulse durations of 20-150ns to selectively ablate unwanted material while preserving the underlying composite substrate. Applications include removal of thermal barrier coatings in aerospace components, de-molding agents in automotive parts, and surface preparation for adhesive bonding. The technology provides significant environmental benefits by eliminating solvent use and reducing energy consumption by 60% compared to traditional methods while achieving contamination removal efficiency exceeding 99.5%."
