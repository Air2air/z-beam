articleBody: Thermoplastic Elastomers (TE) are versatile composite materials with rubber-like elasticity and thermoplastic processability. Laser cleaning offers a non-contact, precise method for removing contaminants, oxidation layers, or residues from TE surfaces without damaging the substrate. This technique is ideal for applications requiring high cleanliness standards, such as medical devices, automotive components, and industrial seals. The process utilizes controlled laser pulses to vaporize unwanted material while preserving the integrity of the underlying TE structure. Key advantages include no chemical waste, reduced mechanical stress, and compatibility with complex geometries. Optimal laser parameters depend on the TE formulation and desired surface finish.
