articleBody: Tempered glass (TG), with its chemical composition SiO₂·Na₂O·CaO, requires specialized cleaning methods to maintain its structural integrity. Laser cleaning offers a precise, non-contact solution to remove dirt, oxides, and residues without compromising the glass surface. This method eliminates the risk of micro-scratches and chemical damage associated with traditional cleaning techniques. Ideal for industrial and architectural applications, laser cleaning ensures long-term durability while preserving optical clarity.
