articleBody: "Rubber laser cleaning offers a precise, non-contact method for removing contaminants from polymer surfaces without compromising material integrity. The process utilizes controlled laser parameters to selectively ablate unwanted substances while preserving the underlying rubber composition C₅H₈. This technique is particularly effective for industrial rubber components, seals, and gaskets where chemical cleaning may cause degradation. Key advantages include no solvent residues, minimal heat affected zones, and compatibility with complex geometries. Applications range from automotive parts to medical device manufacturing."
