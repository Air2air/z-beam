Laser cleaning provides a superior, non-contact method for preparing Aluminum surfaces by precisely removing oxides, oils, paints, and anodized layers without inducing thermal stress or mechanical damage to the soft substrate. The process employs fiber lasers, typically operating at 1064nm wavelength, which are highly absorbed by surface contaminants yet reflected by the pure Aluminum underneath, creating a self-limiting process. This eliminates the need for chemical solvents and abrasive media, offering an environmentally friendly solution that enhances adhesion for subsequent welding, painting, or bonding processes.
