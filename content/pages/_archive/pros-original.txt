Consultation & Assessment
We'll discuss your cleaning objectives, the materials involved, the types of contamination, and any specific requirements or constraints. For more complex projects, an on-site evaluation and material analysis may be necessary.

Proposal & Pricing
Based on the assessment, we'll provide a detailed proposal outlining the project scope, our recommended cleaning methodology, safety protocols, timeline, and a firm price.  We offer flexible pricing options (per square foot, hourly, or project-based) tailored to your project requirements.

Project Plan & Prep
Our team develops a site-specific safety plan, determines optimal laser parameters, and coordinates logistics to minimize disruption to your operations.

Expert Execution
Our highly trained technicians use state-of-the-art laser cleaning equipment, specialized enclosures, and dust collection systems to execute the cleaning with precision and care.  We continuously monitor laser parameters and conduct quality checks throughout the process.

Completion & Report
We provide a comprehensive project report and invoice.
