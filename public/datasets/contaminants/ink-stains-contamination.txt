DESCRIPTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Ink-stains contamination, it arises from pigmented deposits of
industrial printing, markers, and spray paint that adhere
through solvent evaporation and penetration. This pattern
distinguishes itself by irregular pigmentation and strong
bonding, unlike uniform rust or dust layers, and on porous paper
or cardboard, it seeps deeply while on durable steel or
concrete, it forms tenacious surface films. Removal challenges
include chemical resistance and substrate damage risk, thus
laser cleaning excels as pulsed energy vaporizes stains
selectively and restores material integrity without abrasion.
