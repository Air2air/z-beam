───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DESCRIPTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

After pickling process, residue forms from acid and iron salts
on steel surfaces so contamination builds up. This residue shows
crystalline patterns, distinct from rust because it dissolves in
water unlike fixed oxides. On stainless steel, it causes pitting
and laser cleaning removes it effectively by targeted ablation
without damaging substrate.
