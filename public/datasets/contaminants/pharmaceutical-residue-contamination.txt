DESCRIPTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Pharmaceutical-residue contamination, it arises from active
ingredient deposits during equipment processing. This residue,
it forms sticky layers and differs from mineral scales by
organic stickiness that bonds deeply. On stainless steel and
glass, it clings unevenly and defies solvents, so removal
challenges persist; laser cleaning, it vaporizes residue
selectively and leaves surfaces intact.
