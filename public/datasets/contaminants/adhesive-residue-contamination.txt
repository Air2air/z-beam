DATASET: Adhesive Residue / Tape Marks Contamination Removal
================================================================================

DESCRIPTION:
Adhesive residue contamination consists of sticky organic films that form on surfaces after tape or label removal from manufactured items and shipped goods. During handling, this residue re-adheres easily, so it differs from oily contaminants because strong bonding occurs on metals like steel and aluminum, while blending happens into plastics such as ABS. Mechanical removal faces challenges from re-sticking, but laser cleaning vaporizes organics selectively and leaves substrates undamaged.
