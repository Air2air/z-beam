DATASET: Steel Surface Corrosion Contamination Removal
================================================================================

DESCRIPTION:
Steel corrosion contamination, it arises from oxidation on carbon steel surfaces exposed to moisture and air. This rust layer, it adheres tightly and penetrates substrate unlike loose dust or paint, thus poses removal challenges by risking base material damage during mechanical methods. Laser cleaning targets it effectively, as ablation vaporizes oxide precisely and leaves surface intact.
