MAPLE LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: wood > hardwood
Title: Maple Settings
Description: When laser cleaning Maple, I've found gentle pulses work best. This hardwood's tight grain absorbs energy evenly, unlike looser woods that char fast. It brings back finishes on furniture or instruments without splintering. Tends to heat slowly due to low conductivity compared to denser hardwoods. So, keep passes light. Watch for overexposure at the end—it can dull the natural luster if you push too hard.
