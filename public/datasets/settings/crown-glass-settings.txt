Title: Crown Glass Settings
Description: We've found Crown Glass tricky to clean without cracking. It handles heat poorly compared to sturdier borosilicate types. Start low on power to dodge thermal shock.

This soda-lime glass expands quickly when heated. Heat builds up unevenly since it conducts poorly. We adjust by slowing scan speeds and adding overlaps.

Unlike denser ceramics, its fragility demands gentle pulses. Multiple light passes restore surfaces cleanly. Avoid high energy—it warps edges fast.

In heritage work, this approach preserves details well. We've seen it shine in labs too. Just cool between runs to stay safe.
