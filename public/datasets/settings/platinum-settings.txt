PLATINUM LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: metal > non-ferrous
Title: Platinum Settings
Description: When laser cleaning platinum, watch its high reflectivity first. It bounces back most laser energy. This scatters the beam and slows contaminant removal. I've seen jobs drag on without adjustments. Start low on power to build heat gradually. Tends to work best with multiple passes. Overlap scans tightly for even coverage. Avoid high fluence early—it risks uneven heating. Platinum's heat resistance helps here. It stays stable under steady pulses. This restores surfaces cleanly without warping. Just keep scans slow to control buildup.
