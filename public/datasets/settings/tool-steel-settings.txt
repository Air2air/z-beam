Title: Tool Steel Settings
Description: When laser cleaning Tool Steel, watch its toughness first—it resists buildup removal more than softer alloys like mild steel. I've seen overheating cause surface cracks if you rush. Finish with a quick check for smoothness. It restores tools perfectly.
