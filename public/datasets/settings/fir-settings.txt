FIR LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: wood > softwood
Title: Fir Settings
Description: When laser cleaning Fir, watch its softness compared to hardwoods—it chars faster under heat. You must start with low power to prevent surface damage. Unlike denser woods, Fir's high porosity absorbs contaminants deeply, so use multiple gentle passes. This clears grime without warping the grain. Keep scans slow for even coverage. Adjust overlap to avoid hot spots. Fir restores well in heritage work.
