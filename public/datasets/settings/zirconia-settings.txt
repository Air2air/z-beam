ZIRCONIA LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: ceramic > oxide
Title: Zirconia Settings
Description: The key with Zirconia is its strong laser absorption. We've found this makes cleaning efficient without much reflection loss. It removes contaminants quickly while preserving the surface. Unlike metals, its low thermal conductivity traps heat locally. We typically start with moderate power to prevent buildup. This approach reduces cracking risks in such a hard ceramic. Zirconia stands out for high toughness under stress. Adjust scan speeds higher for even exposure. In our experience, multiple light passes restore it fully. Watch for thermal gradients at the end, though, and cool between runs to avoid micro-fractures.
