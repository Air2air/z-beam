POLYPROPYLENE LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: plastic > thermoplastic
Title: Polypropylene Settings
Description: When laser cleaning polypropylene, you'll want to start with the lowest possible power settings to handle its heat sensitivity, which makes it more prone to melting than tougher thermoplastics like ABS. Next, ramp up scan speed gradually while keeping overlaps minimal, as its low thermal conductivity traps heat locally and can cause warping if you linger too long. This approach restores surfaces cleanly without deformation. Just watch out for overexposure—always test on scraps first to avoid softening the material.
