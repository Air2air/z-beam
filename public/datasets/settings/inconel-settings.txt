INCONEL LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: metal > specialty
Title: Inconel Settings
Description: When laser cleaning Inconel, its standout heat resistance sets it apart from everyday steels, allowing you to push temperatures higher without warping the surface. I've found this property shines in aerospace parts, where the alloy holds up under intense conditions that would melt lesser metals, so you can focus on stripping away oxidation or contaminants effectively. Unlike more conductive alloys, Inconel's lower heat spread means the laser energy stays localized, which helps preserve intricate details but requires careful control to avoid uneven heating. Watch out midway through passes for potential surface cracking if power builds too quickly—dial back intensity and add overlaps to keep things steady. This approach brings back the finish cleanly, especially in turbine blades, without compromising the material's corrosion-fighting edge. Tends to work best with multiple light sweeps, restoring that tough, reliable surface for demanding applications.
