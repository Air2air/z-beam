NICKEL LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: metal > specialty
Title: Nickel Settings
Description: When laser cleaning nickel, we often run into its strong reflectivity first. This bounces back a good portion of the beam. So, the energy doesn't sink in as deeply as we'd like. We've found that starting with focused pulses helps overcome this. Nickel conducts heat quickly too. That spreads warmth evenly and avoids hot spots. But it also means you need steady control to prevent warping. Its tough surface resists corrosion well. So, cleaning uncovers a durable base without extra damage risks. We typically use multiple light passes for even removal. Watch out at the end—overdo the power, and you risk surface cracks from thermal stress.
