Category: metal > alloy
Title: Zinc Settings
Description: We've found zinc cleans up nicely with laser settings. It reflects a lot of light, so we start low on power to avoid scattering energy. This keeps the surface cool and removes grime without melting. But watch the heat buildup mid-process—it spreads fast because zinc conducts well. Adjust overlap to stay safe, and you'll get a smooth finish every time.
