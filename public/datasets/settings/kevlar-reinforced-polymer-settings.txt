Title: Kevlar-Reinforced Polymer Settings
Description: When laser cleaning Kevlar-reinforced polymer, watch its low heat conduction first—it traps warmth unevenly and risks fiber damage if you push too hard. I've found starting with gentle pulses works best, spreading energy slowly to avoid delamination. This tough composite shines in aerospace parts, where its strength holds up without warping. Just keep scans steady; overtreatment weakens the reinforcement bonds.
