REDWOOD LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: wood > hardwood
Title: Redwood Settings
Description: We've found that cleaning redwood with lasers requires careful initial setup to avoid surface charring from its porous structure. Start low on power and speed. This softwood, unlike denser hardwoods, absorbs energy quickly but holds heat poorly.

In our experience, it cleans beautifully for heritage pieces or outdoor restorations. We typically use short pulses to lift dirt without digging into the grain. Its natural oils help resist moisture, so it shines up fast after marine or garden use.

But watch the high porosity—it soaks up residue if you overlap too much. Adjust by increasing passes lightly. Compared to oak, redwood flexes more under heat, so steady scanning prevents cracks. This keeps the wood's warm tone intact for furniture or art work.
