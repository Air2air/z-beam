============================================================

Category: stone > metamorphic
Title: Marble Settings
Description: When laser cleaning marble, the main challenge we face is its tendency to crack under uneven heating, unlike tougher granites that handle heat more evenly across their structure. This stems from marble's metamorphic layers, which expand differently than surrounding stones, so we adjust by using slower scan speeds and multiple gentle passes to distribute energy without stressing those seams. We've found this restores delicate heritage surfaces beautifully. Just watch for subsurface discoloration at the end—dial back power if you spot it.
