TEAK LASER CLEANING SETTINGS

Category: wood > hardwood
Title: Teak Settings
Description: When laser cleaning teak, you'll want to start by focusing on its distinctive oil-rich structure, which sets it apart from drier hardwoods like oak by enhancing laser absorption without rapid heat buildup. This property allows the process to remove surface contaminants effectively while preserving the wood's natural grain and color. Make sure you maintain a steady, controlled scan to avoid uneven charring, as teak's moderate density can lead to localized overheating if speeds vary. Compared to softer woods, teak restores well under gentle passes that expose underlying layers cleanly, reducing the risk of splintering. Adjust dwell times longer than for less absorbent materials to ensure thorough cleaning without compromising its inherent durability.
