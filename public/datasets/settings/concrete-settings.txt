CONCRETE LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: masonry > general
Title: Concrete Settings
Description: We've found that concrete demands initial low-power passes in laser cleaning to avoid aggregate spalling, a risk not as prevalent in denser stones like granite. Its inherent porosity allows effective absorption of laser energy, which reveals embedded contaminants without excessive heat buildup. We typically follow with multiple overlapping scans to restore surface uniformity in infrastructure settings, reducing the chance of uneven ablation.
