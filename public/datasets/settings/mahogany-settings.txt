MAHOGANY LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: wood > hardwood
Title: Mahogany Settings
Description: When laser cleaning mahogany, start with a careful assessment of its high porosity, which can lead to uneven energy absorption if not managed. This property requires you to reduce power levels initially to prevent surface charring while ensuring the laser removes contaminants without embedding them deeper into the wood's open structure. Adjust scan speeds higher than for denser hardwoods to minimize heat buildup, restoring the material's natural grain effectively and avoiding cracks from thermal stress.
