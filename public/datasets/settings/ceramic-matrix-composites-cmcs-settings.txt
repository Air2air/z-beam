In our experience, this approach leverages the material's durability and low expansion under heat, ensuring clean results for automotive or energy components while dodging common pitfalls like subsurface damage from over-aggressive pulses. Finally, inspect between passes to confirm no oxidation creeps in, adjusting as needed for optimal preservation.
