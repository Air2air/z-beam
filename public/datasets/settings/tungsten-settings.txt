TUNGSTEN LASER CLEANING SETTINGS
============================================================

Category: metal > alloy
Title: Tungsten Settings
Description: When laser cleaning Tungsten, you'll want to start with a low-power setup to handle its high reflectivity, which scatters much of the beam and slows contaminant removal. Make sure you use short pulses and slow scan speeds, because this dense, heat-resistant material absorbs energy unevenly—watch out for surface pitting if you ramp up too fast. Adjust overlap to cover spots fully, and finish with multiple passes to clear buildup without weakening the tough structure.
