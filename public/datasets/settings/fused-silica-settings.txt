Title: Fused Silica Settings
Description: When laser cleaning fused silica, the biggest challenge is its low absorption of laser energy. This makes the process slower since the beam tends to pass through the material with minimal interaction. I've seen that pulsing at moderate rates removes surface contaminants effectively without overheating. Multiple gentle passes reveal the underlying clarity. Adjust scan speeds to avoid thermal buildup, which can lead to cracking.
