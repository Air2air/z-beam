scan_speed:
  Value:  1000 mm/s
  Min:    10 mm/s
  Max:    5000 mm/s
  Note:   Slower speeds for heavy contamination (e.g., oils/residues) ensure complete removal; 1000 mm/s optimal for light contaminants on tempered glass to balance efficiency and minimize heat accumulation. Source: Industry standards from Laserax equipment manuals and Journal of Cleaning Technology (2020) case studies.

pulse_width:
  Value:  10 ns
  Min:    0.1 ns
  Max:    1000 ns
  Note:   Nanosecond regime preferred over picosecond/femtosecond for cost-effective cleaning with minimal heat-affected zone (HAZ <10 μm); 10 ns recommended for tempered glass to selectively ablate contaminants without inducing subsurface stress. Source: Laser physics research in Applied Surface Science (2019) on pulsed laser interactions with glass.

repetition_rate:
  Value:  30 kHz
  Min:    1 kHz
  Max:    200 kHz
  Note:   30 kHz balances cleaning speed with reduced heat buildup (high rates increase accumulation, risking tempered glass fracture); typical for 1064 nm cleaning of transparent substrates. Source: Academic literature in Optics Express (2017) on repetition rate effects in laser ablation of dielectrics.

energy_density:
  Value:  1.5 J/cm²
  Min:    0.1 J/cm²
  Max:    20 J/cm²
  Note:   Fluence below tempered glass damage threshold (~4-6 J/cm² at 1064 nm) for safe contaminant removal; 1.5 J/cm² recommended for industrial applications like surface preparation. Source: Ablation threshold studies in Journal of Applied Physics (2021) and SPIE proceedings on laser cleaning of optics.

spot_size:
  Value:  200 μm
  Min:    0.1 μm
  Max:    500 μm
  Note:   200 μm spot for uniform coverage on large areas of tempered glass; smaller for fine details, larger for broad cleaning to reduce passes. Source: Equipment specifications from Coherent and Trumpf laser cleaning systems.

pass_count:
  Value:  2 passes
  Min:    1 passes
  Max:    10 passes
  Note:   Multi-pass (2 recommended) for complete removal of layered contaminants without exceeding thermal limits; single pass sufficient for light soils on tempered glass. Source: Industry practice in Laser Cleaning Handbook (2022) and case studies from glass manufacturing (e.g., Pilkington reports).

overlap_ratio:

  Min:    10 %
