  Value:  20 kHz
  Min:    1 kHz
  Max:    200 kHz
  Note:   Typical for industrial cleaning; 20 kHz balances speed and heat accumulation on reflective SiGe surfaces (absorptivity ~0.4 at 1064 nm); higher rates risk buildup, lower for heavy cleaning.
