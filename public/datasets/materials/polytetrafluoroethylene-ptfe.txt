DATASET: Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) Laser Cleaning Parameters

DESCRIPTION:
Polytetrafluoroethylene exhibits strong chemical resistance and low friction. For laser cleaning, it allows precise contaminant removal without residue, thus preserves surface integrity in demanding fields like aerospace and medical devices. Thermal sensitivity poses challenges, so controlled processing follows to avoid deformation.
