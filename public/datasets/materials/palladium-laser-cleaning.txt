  Value:  1500 mm/s
  Min:    10 mm/s

  Note:   Effective speeds for contamination removal on Pd; slower for heavy oxides (500-1000 mm/s), faster for light contaminants (2000-3000 mm/s). Recommended 1500 mm/s for optimal balance without excessive heat. Source: Industry standards from Laserax manuals; J. Cleaner Prod. (2020) case studies on metal surface prep.

pulse_width:
  Value:  20 ns
  Min:    0.1 ns
  Max:    1000 ns
  Note:   Ns regime preferred for cost-effective cleaning of Pd; ps (5-50 ps) minimizes HAZ due to Pd's thermal conductivity (71 W/mK), but ns sufficient. Avoid fs for industrial scale. Recommended 20 ns for low thermal impact. Source: Laser physics in Appl. Phys. A (2015) on metal ablation thresholds.

repetition_rate:
  Value:  50 kHz
  Min:    1 kHz
  Max:    200 kHz
  Note:   Typical for pulsed cleaning to control heat accumulation on reflective Pd; higher rates speed up process but risk buildup. 50 kHz recommended for uniform cleaning without overheating. Trade-off: >80 kHz increases efficiency but requires cooling. Source: Academic lit. in J. Appl. Phys. (2017); Coherent Inc. equipment guidelines.
