  Value:  20 ns
  Min:    0.1 ns
  Max:    1000 ns
  Note:   Nanosecond regime preferred for industrial cleaning to minimize heat-affected zone (HAZ) in transparent lead crystal at 1064 nm; picosecond (ps) or femtosecond reduces thermal effects but increases cost.
