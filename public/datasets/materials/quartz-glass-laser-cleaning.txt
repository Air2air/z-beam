  Value:  500 mm/s

  Note:   Effective range for 1064 nm cleaning; slower (100-500 mm/s) for heavy contamination, faster (1000-2000 mm/s) for light; 500 mm/s recommended for optimal balance on quartz to minimize heat input (source: Industry standards from Laserax manuals and Opt.   Value:  20 ns
  Min:    0.1 ns
  Max:    1000 ns
  Note:   Nanosecond regime typical for cost-effective cleaning of quartz glass; 5-50 ns for ns lasers, ps (10-100 ps) for minimal HAZ but higher cost; 20 ns recommended to reduce thermal effects given quartz's low absorption at 1064 nm (source: Laser physics in Appl. Phys. A 2019 on pulse regimes for dielectrics).

repetition_rate:
  Value:  50 kHz
  Min:    1 kHz
  Max:    200 kHz
  Note:   Standard for pulsed cleaning; 20-50 kHz for controlled energy delivery, up to 100 kHz for faster processing but risk of heat accumulation on low-conductivity quartz; 50 kHz balances speed and safety (source: Academic lit. in J. Appl. Phys. 2021 on rep rate trade-offs for transparent substrates).

energy_density:
  Value:  1.5 J/cm²
  Min:    0.1 J/cm²
  Max:    20 J/cm²
  Note:   Fluence for contaminant ablation without substrate damage; damage threshold for quartz ~10-15 J/cm² at 1064 nm ns pulses; 1.5 J/cm² recommended for safe cleaning of organics/metals on glass (source: Ablation studies in Opt. Express 2017 on fused silica thresholds).

pass_count:
  Value:  2 passes
  Min:    1 passes
  Max:    10 passes
