repetition_rate:
  Value:  30 kHz
  Min:    1 kHz
  Max:    200 kHz
  Note:   Typical for 1064 nm cleaning; higher rates (>50 kHz) speed up process but risk heat accumulation on low-conductivity Zr. 30 kHz balances throughput and safety. Trade-off: faster cleaning vs. potential subsurface heating. Source: Academic literature on pulsed laser cleaning (Appl. Surf. Sci. 2021).
