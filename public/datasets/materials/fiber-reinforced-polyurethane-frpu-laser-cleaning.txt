  Value:  1500 mm/s

  Value:  50 ns
  Min:    0.1 ns
  Max:    1000 ns
  Note:   Nanosecond regime preferred for cost-effective cleaning of polymers; picosecond (ps) or femtosecond (fs) for ultra-minimal HAZ but overkill for FRPU. 50 ns minimizes heat-affected zone while ablating contaminants.   Value:  40 kHz
  Min:    1 kHz
  Max:    200 kHz
  Note:   Typical for pulsed fiber lasers in cleaning; 40 kHz recommended for FRPU to enable fast processing without excessive heat accumulation in low-thermal-conductivity material. Higher rates (>60 kHz) risk buildup. Source: Academic literature on composite laser processing (Composites Part B, 2019).

energy_density:
  Value:  0.8 J/cm²

  Note:   Fluence range for safe contaminant removal on FRPU; damage threshold ~1.5-2.5 J/cm² due to polyurethane's low melting point (~180-250°C) and moderate 1064 nm absorption. Recommended 0.8 J/cm² for cleaning without substrate harm. Source: Ablation threshold studies (Appl. Surf. Sci. 2017).
