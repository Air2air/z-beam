  Max:    5000 mm/s

  Value:  20 ns
  Min:    0.1 ns
  Max:    1000 ns
  Note:   Nanosecond regime typical for industrial cleaning to minimize heat-affected zone (HAZ) on heat-sensitive Onyx; 20 ns recommended over ps/fs for cost-effectiveness while reducing thermal accumulation (laser physics in Opt. Lasers Eng. 2020).

repetition_rate:
  Value:  30 kHz
  Min:    1 kHz
  Max:    200 kHz
  Note:   Common for 1064 nm cleaning; 30 kHz recommended for Onyx to balance speed and heat buildup (high freq risks accumulation on low-conductivity substrates); trade-off noted in Appl. Surf. Sci. 2019.

  Value:  1.5 J/cm²

  Note:   Fluence range for safe contaminant ablation; Onyx damage threshold ~6-8 J/cm² (SiO2-based); 1.5 J/cm² recommended for cleaning without substrate etching (ablation studies in J. Appl. Phys. 2017).

spot_size:
